How Do I Start?

Simply visit out 'Contact' page and send us a message! Provide as much detail about what you want to work on improving, and we will get back to you as soon as possible through your preference of a phone call or email.

How It Works:

We meet all of our new clients for a free one hour evaluation session. The purpose of this session is to get to know each other, and to better understand what goals you are trying to reach as a player. We assess your current capabilities and really get a feel for how we can most effectively instruct you to being your best. There is no obligation and no hassle.

Once training begins, we meet regularly with our clients and work on improving specific skill sets. We always send our players home with 'homework' to help them improve on their own time.

Payment is accepted through cash or Venmo.™ We offer a $5.00 per session discount for payments made in cash. We are very flexible, and can work with you to form payment plans if need be. There is no contract and no obligation. Our only rule is that a 24-hour cancellation notice needs to be given for missing training at no charge.
